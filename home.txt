<article><i><b>Mr. Robot</b></i> is an American <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thriller_(genre)" title="Thriller (genre)">drama thriller</a> television series created by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Esmail" title="Sam Esmail">Sam Esmail</a> for <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/USA_Network" title="USA Network">USA Network</a>. It stars <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rami_Malek" title="Rami Malek">Rami Malek</a> as Elliot Alderson, a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_security" title="Computer security">cybersecurity</a> engineer and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Security_hacker" title="Security hacker">hacker</a> with <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_anxiety_disorder" title="Social anxiety disorder">social anxiety disorder</a> and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Clinical_depression" class="mw-redirect" title="Clinical depression">clinical depression</a>. Elliot is recruited by an <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Insurrectionary_anarchism" title="Insurrectionary anarchism">insurrectionary anarchist</a> known as "Mr. Robot", played by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_Slater" title="Christian Slater">Christian Slater</a>, to join a group of <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hacktivism" title="Hacktivism">hacktivists</a> called "fsociety". The group aims to destroy all debt records by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Encryption" title="Encryption">encrypting</a> the financial data of E Corp, the largest <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conglomerate_(company)" title="Conglomerate (company)">conglomerate</a> in the world.



</p><p>The pilot premiered via online and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Video_on_demand" title="Video on demand">video on demand</a> services on May 27, 2015. The first season debuted on USA Network on June 24, 2015, and the second on July 13, 2016. The third season premiered on October 11, 2017. The fourth and final season premiered on October 6, 2019, and concluded on December 22, 2019.



</p><p><i>Mr. Robot</i> has received critical acclaim: it won the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_Globe_Award_for_Best_Television_Series_%E2%80%93_Drama" title="Golden Globe Award for Best Television Series – Drama">Golden Globe for Best Television Drama Series</a>; it was recognized with a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peabody_Award" title="Peabody Award">Peabody Award</a>; and in 2016, the series received six <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Awards" class="mw-redirect" title="Primetime Emmy Awards">Emmy</a> nominations, including Outstanding Drama Series and Malek winning for <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Primetime_Emmy_Award_for_Outstanding_Lead_Actor_in_a_Drama_Series" title="Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series">Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series</a>.
